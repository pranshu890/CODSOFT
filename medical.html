Why Choose Us

It is the people who make Medicenter what it is and we are extremely proud of the achievements of our staff. We all work together to help our patients through recovery, providing the best possible care.

Outpatient Service

We provide comprehensive outpatient assistance and emergency care without the need for prior registration.

Hospital treatment

We have a fully equipped modern hospital ward where we take care of every mother and her children.

Child Oriented

Our children's needs always come first. Smiling staff, colorful and modern rooms and offices, healthy and tasty cuisine.

Family Planing

We know how important the family is, so we will take care of you and provide you with a professional approach.

Midwife Care

Our midwives will accompany you even when you leave our care center. They will provide the necessary support.

Perinatal

Perinatal care is important to every mother. We will provide you with support and knowledge at this important time.
